About Us

Our Mission

At Workout Planner, we aim to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals through tailored workout plans and expert guidance. Our mission is to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their experience level. Founded by a group of fitness enthusiasts, Workout Planner was born out of a desire to create a platform where people can find the motivation and resources they need to stay active and healthy.

Project Goals

  • We want to make the platform as easy as it can be to navigate for our user's.
  • We want to help user's stay on track with their fitness goals through reminders and tracking progress.
  • We want to encourage our user's to take charge of their fitness journeys and help guide them to their goals.

Values

  • We value our user's in everything we do, and try to provide a well-balanced workout plan for them.
  • We value our user's time. The time they have to schedule and plan their workouts can be spent doing more important things because of us.
  • We value user's of all fitness levels, beginners, athletes, or experienced user's.
