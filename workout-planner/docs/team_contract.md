Project Team Name: Workouts Planner 
------------------------------------


A. Team Structure 
==================

### 1. Leadership structure (individual, individual with rotating leadership, shared):
    Individual with rotating leadership 

    Leader is responsible for the following: 
    Responsible for submitting any assignments due
    Responsible for oversight and organization of work
    Point of contact for the team

### 2. Decision-making policy: 
    By majority vote

### 3. Day, time, and place for regular team meetings:
    Depends on workload, usually sunday afternoon (once a week)

### 4. Usual method of communication (e.g., e-mail, cell phone, wired phone, in person): 
    SMS through cell phone

B. Team Procedures 
===================

### 1. Method for setting and following meeting agendas (Who will set each agenda? When? How will team
### members be notified/reminded? Who will be responsible for the team following the agenda during a
### team meeting?):
    Leader's job to communicate through the group chat in case of emergency. Leader is responsible for following the agenda during the meeting. 
    Every tuesday leader is supposed to set up the agenda for the next meeting. 

### 2. Method of record keeping (Who will be responsible for recording and disseminating minutes? How
### and when will the minutes be disseminated? Where will all agendas and minutes be stored?):
    Leader is responsible for the record keeping in a separate Markdown file where the agendas for the meetings will be stored.
    Leader is responsible for doing that on Tuesday as well since there will be a planning of the next meeting during that time. 

### 3. Procedures in the absence of a team member: (will the team meet with one member absent, or must
### all members be present?): 
    Team members can meet with each other with one being absent. In that case, an email of updates will be sent to the one who missed the meeting. 
    In case of an absence of two team members, then the meeting will be postponed once to the other day at least two days prior the deadline. 
    It is the leader's responsibility to get in touch with the team members in case of their absence on the postponed 
    meeting to keep them with the up-to-date info on the project. Leader has to make sure that the team members who missed the meetings, should not be left behind.

C. Team Participation
=====================

### 1. Strategies to ensure cooperation and equal distribution of tasks:
    Tasks for each week will be in the markdown file with agenda and minutes. There, the tasks will be divided equally into five pieces, 
    so that each team member could get equal task in terms of how hard it is, and how comfortable for a team member it could be doing it.

### 2. Strategies for encouraging/including ideas from all team members (team maintenance): 
    Every team member will be asked by a leader during the meeting if they all agree with the details provided. 
    There will be voting to make a final decision. If someone disagrees, then that person will be able to state own position.

D. Personal Accountability
==========================

### 1. Expected individual attendance, punctuality, and participation at all team meetings:
    Every team member is expected to be present on every team meeting on time.

### 2. Expected level of responsibility for fulfilling team assignments, timelines, and deadlines:
    Everyone is responsible for the assigned task to complete at the given time.

### 3. Expected level of communication with other team members:
    Every team member is expected to communicate what they plan to do and how they plan to do it.
    There should be 24-hour notice if a team member is going to miss the meeting.

### 4. Expected level of commitment to team decisions and tasks: 
    Every team member is expected to provide with any sort of feedback on what was discussed during the team meetings.
    Every team member's work/task completed will be evaluated and given a feedback on a weekly meetings by other team members.

E. Consequences of breach of contract:
======================================

### 1. Policies for handling infractions of any of the obligations of this team contract:
    Leader is responsible for contacting that person, asking the reason for the infraction of the obligations stated in the document. 
    A team member that made the infraction will be given a grace at the first time for a reasonable excuse.
    In case the strategies stated above do not work, then leader is responsible to communicate with the professor.
